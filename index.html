Project Mission

We aim to share our workspace’s CNC and metal laser cutting facilities with teams that rely on external resources for manufacturing. By producing needed robot parts in-house, we want to make the process faster and more efficient for everyone. With Build Together, our goal is to strengthen collaboration between teams, share production experience, and create more sustainable solutions together. This project is initiated and made by team #6431, NoktaParantez.
